Common Questions & Solutions

  • "Hey Siri" doesn't work: Make sure it's enabled in Settings ‚Üí Siri & Search. Re-train Siri to recognize your voice if needed.
  • Siri doesn't understand me: Speak clearly and naturally. Check that your microphone isn't blocked or covered.
  • Siri gives wrong contact/info: Check your contacts and make sure relationships are set correctly (Settings ‚Üí Contacts ‚Üí My Info).
  • HomePod won't control devices: Make sure devices are added to the Home app and assigned to the correct room.
  • Siri is too loud/quiet: Adjust volume with "Hey Siri, turn the volume to 50%" or use the volume buttons.
