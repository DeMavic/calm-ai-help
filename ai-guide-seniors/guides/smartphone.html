Getting Started

How to Activate Your Assistant

1

Wake Up Your Assistant

iPhone (Siri): Press and hold the side button (or home button on older iPhones) until you see the Siri screen, OR just say "Hey Siri"

Android (Google Assistant): Press and hold the home button (circle at bottom), OR say "Hey Google" or "OK Google"

üí° Tip: The voice activation ("Hey Siri" or "Hey Google") is super convenient! You can enable it in your phone's settings under Siri or Google Assistant.
2

Try Your First Command

Once your assistant is listening (you'll see the screen light up), try asking a simple question:

"What's the weather today?"

Your assistant will answer out loud and show you the result on screen!

3

That's It!

Seriously, it's that simple. You activate, you ask, you get an answer. The more you practice, the easier it gets.

Common Questions & Solutions

  • My assistant doesn't understand me: Speak clearly and at a normal pace. It's okay to repeat yourself! You can also try phrasing your question differently.
  • "Hey Siri" or "Hey Google" doesn't work: You may need to enable voice activation in Settings. Look for Siri & Search (iPhone) or Google Assistant (Android).
  • I don't know what to ask: Start with simple things like weather, timers, or asking questions. The more you use it, the more ideas you'll have!
  • It gave me the wrong answer: That's okay! AI isn't perfect. You can ask again in a different way, or just try a different question.
  • I'm worried about privacy: You're in control! You can turn off the microphone whenever you want, and delete your voice history in settings.
