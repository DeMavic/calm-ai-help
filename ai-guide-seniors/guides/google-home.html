Getting Started

How to Talk to Google Assistant

1

Wake Phrases

To activate Google Assistant, say one of these phrases:

Hey Google...
OK Google...

You'll hear a chime and see lights on your device‚Äîthat means it's listening!

2

Once it's listening, ask your question clearly:

Google will respond with the answer!

3

Follow-Up Questions

One cool thing about Google Assistant: you can ask follow-up questions without saying "Hey Google" again!

(Google responds)

It remembers the context of your conversation!

Common Questions & Solutions

  • Google doesn't respond: Check that it's plugged in and connected to Wi-Fi. Try moving closer or speaking louder.
  • It doesn't understand me: Speak clearly and wait for the chime before talking. Rephrasing your question can help too!
  • Music won't play: Make sure you've linked a music service (YouTube Music, Spotify, Pandora) in the Google Home app under Settings ‚Üí Music.
  • Can't control smart home devices: Devices must be on the same Wi-Fi network. Try unlinking and re-linking the device in the Google Home app.
  • Wrong person's calendar/info: Set up Voice Match so Google knows who's talking!
