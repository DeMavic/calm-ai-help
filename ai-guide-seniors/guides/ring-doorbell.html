Common Questions

What if I don't hear the doorbell ring inside my house?

You can buy a Ring Chime (a plug-in device) that rings inside your home when someone presses the doorbell. It's about $30 and super easy to set up.

Can I see the video on my computer?

Yes! Go to ring.com and log in with your account. You can view live video and recordings from any web browser.

What if my Wi-Fi is slow?

Ring needs good Wi-Fi to work well. If your doorbell is far from your router, consider a Wi-Fi extender or mesh system.

Do I need a subscription?

No! You can see live video and answer the door for free. A Ring Protect subscription ($4/month) adds video recording history and extra features.
