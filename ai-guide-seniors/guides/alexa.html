Getting Started

How to Talk to Alexa

1

To get Alexa's attention, just say "Alexa" and wait for the blue light ring to appear on your Echo device.

You'll hear a tone and see the light ring turn blue‚Äîthat means she's listening!

Smart Home Control

One of Alexa's superpowers is controlling smart home devices‚Äîlights, thermostats, plugs, and more!

Setting Up Smart Devices

1

Discover Devices

After setting up a smart device (like a smart bulb or plug), tell Alexa to find it:

Alexa, discover my devices

Alexa will search for new smart home devices and add them automatically.

2

Name Your Devices

In the Alexa app, give each device a simple, memorable name like "Living Room Lamp" or "Bedroom Light."

üí° Tip: Use names that are easy to say and remember!

Common Questions & Solutions

  • Alexa doesn't respond: Make sure she's plugged in and the light ring works. Try saying "Alexa" louder or from closer.
  • She misunderstands me: Speak clearly and at a normal pace. You can also check what Alexa heard in the Alexa app under Settings ‚Üí Alexa Privacy ‚Üí Review Voice History.
  • Music won't play: Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi and have a music service set up (Amazon Music, Spotify, etc.) in the Alexa app.
  • Smart home device not found: Make sure the device is powered on and connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your Echo. Try "Alexa, discover my devices" again.
  • Volume is too loud/quiet: Say "Alexa, volume 5" (1-10 scale) to set the perfect level.
